How We Take Care of Cheese

Cows make cheese

Cheese is the Devil's Lettuce

First we milk the cow. Then we drink the milk. Then we milk the cow again, making sure to not drink it. We put the milk in the fridge and leave it there for 3 years. We take it out, scrape off the visible mold, and then package it. It's perfection.
